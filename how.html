How it works

How points work

Cucumber points are based on the impact of the brands you buy, compared with similar brands. If you buy a big brand with a better impact than another big brand, you’ll be rewarded.

How we work

Cucumber uses the latest science and economic data to quantify which brands have a better impact compared to similar brands.

Why?

There are real differences between brands you’re actually choosing between. You’ll instantly see that difference with your Cucumber score.
